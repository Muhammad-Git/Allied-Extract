The Team

Lydia N.

Executive Director

A medical professional by day and enabler of non profits by night, Lydia has been behind the evacuation of hundreds of high risk Afghans since the fall of GIRoA in Aug 2021.

Lydia is helping us develop Allied Extract into a world class organization that will be able to find a way wherever we need to help.

Brad G.

Director of Operations

A retired 22 year US Navy Seebee that served across the world with a background in getting-it-done at every level and a published author who has led the highly successful veterans on the Camino 501c3, Brad is bringing us forward, building us into an organization that will serve in perpetuity.

Aaron D.

Co-Founder

A Marine’s Marine with multiple combat tours of Afghanistan behind him, he led through adversity, including fighting in Sangin, the “Hell of Helmand.” Now a Police Detective, when he’s not investigating a crime, he leads our operations and ensures our people make it home every night. 021.

Harvey G.

Co-Founder

A former British Army Reconnaissance Specialist with multiple combat tours of Afghanistan behind him, he now calls the US home. He’s worked across Team Rubicon leading Disaster Response Operations in the USA, helped run a $1.3bn Government Program, and now works for a tech company. He builds our capacity and our network.
